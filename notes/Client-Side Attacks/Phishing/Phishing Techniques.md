### MFA Bypass Techniques

#### Prompt Bombing
> Flood target with MFA push notifications until they approve one (**MFA fatigue**).

#### Browser-in-the-Middle
> Proxy real session to capture credentials AND MFA token. Tools like **cuddlephish** automate this.

#### Real-Time Relay
> Capture MFA token and immediately relay to legitimate service before it expires.

#### Social Engineering
> Call target posing as IT/helpdesk and ask for MFA code directly.
